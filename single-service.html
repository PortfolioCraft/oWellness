About me

My name is Dr. Keilah Ford, I am a Doctor of Nursing Practice and dually Board-Certified Psychiatric and Mental Health Nurse Practitioner and Family Nurse Practitioner. I believe in the balance of spiritual, mental, social, physical, and emotional well-being and I go above and beyond by making sure they all align in my treatment approach. I am a motivator, educator, and advocate for my patients and take much pride in doing such roles. Health is wealth and it all starts with a healthy mental state. My motto is “Be kind to your mind.”
