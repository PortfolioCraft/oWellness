About Me

Hi there! I am a Doctor of Nursing Practice and dually Board-Certified Psychiatric and Mental Health Nurse Practitioner and Family Nurse Practitioner. I believe in the balance of spiritual, mental, social, physical, and emotional well-being and I go above and beyond by making sure they all align in my treatment approach.

I am a motivator, educator, and advocate for my patients and take much pride in doing such roles. Health is wealth and it all starts with a healthy mental state. My motto is “Be kind to your mind.”

My Biography

I am Dr. Keilah Ford, a Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner, a Family Nurse Practitioner, dedicated to embracing holistic mental health care. From a young age, I felt a calling to nurture and assist others, inspiring me to pursue nursing.Seeking to make a difference in mental health, I obtained my DNP and specialized in psychiatric care, while also becoming a certified Family Nurse Practitioner. My treatment approach revolves around integrating spiritual, mental, social, physical, and emotional aspects, fostering open communication and trust with my patients.

My motto, "Be kind to your mind," reflects my commitment to promoting mental well-being and creating a compassionate society.
